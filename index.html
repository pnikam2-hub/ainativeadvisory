Agentic PMO

A structured delivery framework that combines AI-native maturity assessment, multi-domain project management, and agent-driven execution — enabling organisations to move from strategy to measurable outcomes.

📋

What It Is

Agentic PMO is a domain-based service delivery architecture built on an AI-native maturity foundation. It deploys structured project packs — domain packs — across up to 10 sectors including manufacturing, governance, financial services, healthcare, education, energy, supply chain, smart cities, professional services, and defence. Each pack follows a three-phase lifecycle: Assess → Build → Deploy.

⚙️

How It Works

Every engagement begins with the 10-dimension maturity diagnostic to establish baselines and identify gaps. Domain packs are then configured — each containing WBS, risk register, KPIs, governance checklist, and delivery milestones. AI agents handle repetitive coordination, reporting, and monitoring, freeing leadership for strategic decisions. A unified dashboard provides real-time visibility across all active workstreams.

🎯

Why It Matters

Traditional PMOs are document-heavy, reactive, and disconnected from strategic AI readiness. Agentic PMO is proactive, measurable, and embedded in the AI-native operating model from day one. It delivers transparency, accountability, and acceleration — turning strategy into governed execution at speed.

Market Focus

FTSE 350 Advisory Practice

We work with FTSE 100 and FTSE 250 leaders to assess AI-native maturity and design transformation programmes that deliver measurable business outcomes.

The Opportunity

The UK's AI adoption paradox: high investment, low organisational transformation. Most FTSE 100 and FTSE 250 companies deploy AI tactically — but few have redesigned their operating models for the age of human-led machine intelligence. Being AI-native means the operating model itself — workflows, decisions, knowledge flows, governance — is built around human-led machine intelligence, not just bolting AI onto legacy processes.

We help close this gap through structured diagnostics, governance frameworks, and delivery roadmaps tailored to the scale and regulatory complexity of FTSE 350 enterprises.

Typical engagement:

Phase 1: Readiness diagnostic (4-6 weeks) — scored maturity profile across 10 dimensions
Phase 2: Governance pack + workflow redesign (6-8 weeks) — designed for your operating context
Phase 3: 90-day transformation roadmap with partner-led implementation support

Why the FTSE 350?

The FTSE 350 are the UK's economic backbone — 100 blue-chip leaders and 250 mid-cap growth engines. They have the resources to lead and a pressing need to transform. Our methodology is built for their scale: regulated, board-governed, and multi-stakeholder.

Our approach

We combine diagnostic rigour with practical delivery. Every engagement starts with data — not opinion. We map your current maturity, identify the highest-leverage gaps, and design a governed programme to close them. We work alongside your teams, not in a separate room.
